# Cost & Usage

## What belongs here

Patterns for attributing AI system spend to models, users, features, prompts, and workflows: token accounting, cost-per-successful-outcome tracking, caching impact measurement, and budget/anomaly alerting — the observability discipline specifically for catching unit-economics regressions (a prompt change, a retry loop, a routing escalation) before they compound.

## What does NOT belong here

General latency/quality monitoring belongs in `monitoring-alerting/` unless the signal is specifically cost-shaped. A specific tool's pricing page or a general "which observability tool has the best cost dashboard" comparison belongs in `content/tools/` or `content/architectures/evaluation-strategy/choose-observability-tool.md`.
