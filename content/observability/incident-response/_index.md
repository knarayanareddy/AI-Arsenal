# Incident Response

## What belongs here

Runbooks and operational playbooks for responding once monitoring/alerting has detected a problem: triage procedures, rollback mechanics, kill-switches for autonomous agent actions, and postmortem structure specific to AI system failure modes (hallucination spikes, retrieval regressions, cost blowouts, runaway agent loops).

## What does NOT belong here

Detection (SLOs, alert rules, dashboards) belongs in `monitoring-alerting/` — this category starts once an incident is already known and focuses on response, not detection.
