# Evaluation & Quality

## What belongs here

Operational playbooks for measuring AI system quality in production: offline/online evaluation loops, golden dataset construction and promotion from production failures, LLM-as-judge calibration against human review, and drift detection — the instrumentation and workflow side of evaluation, not the underlying decision framework for choosing an evaluation approach.

## What does NOT belong here

The decision framework for choosing between deterministic assertions, LLM-as-judge, and human review belongs in `content/architectures/evaluation-strategy/choose-eval-framework.md` — this category assumes that decision is made and focuses on operationalizing it (what to capture, how to gate releases, how to detect drift). A specific evaluation tool's feature comparison belongs in `content/tools/` or `content/architectures/evaluation-strategy/`.
