# Monitoring & Alerting

## What belongs here

Patterns for turning traces and captured signals into operational monitoring: SLOs, alert rules, canary deployments, regression monitors, and dashboards — the layer that tells an on-call engineer something is wrong before a user reports it.

## What does NOT belong here

What to capture belongs in `instrumentation/`; how to assemble it into traces belongs in `tracing/`. What to do once an alert fires belongs in `incident-response/`, not here — this category is about detection, not response.
