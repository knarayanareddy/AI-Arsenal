# Privacy & Governance

## What belongs here

Patterns for handling sensitive data within observability systems themselves: PII detection and redaction in captured prompts/responses, retention policy design, access control for raw traces/logs, and compliance checklists — the discipline that keeps an observability system from becoming a second, ungoverned copy of sensitive production data.

## What does NOT belong here

Application-level data handling (e.g. how a RAG pipeline handles PII in source documents) belongs elsewhere in the catalog; this category is specifically about the observability/telemetry pipeline's own handling of sensitive data captured for debugging and monitoring purposes.
