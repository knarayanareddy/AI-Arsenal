## Overview

TLDR AI is the AI edition of the TLDR newsletter network: a weekday email digest summarizing model releases, research papers, and AI-engineering news in one-to-two-sentence items with links to primary sources.

## Who it's for

Practitioners and founders who want a fast daily radar across the whole AI landscape without following dozens of feeds; also useful for beginners building awareness of the ecosystem's moving parts.

## What you'll get

- A weekday digest covering headline releases (models, tools, funding), 2-3 research paper highlights, and a set of engineering-focused links
- One-to-two-sentence summaries per item, optimized for a 5-minute skim with links out to primary sources
- Coverage breadth that reliably includes open-source releases alongside big-lab announcements

## How to get value fast

Subscribe by email and treat each issue as a triage pass: skim headlines, open only the primary sources relevant to your stack. The value is coverage and cadence, not analysis -- pair it with a deeper weekly read (e.g. Interconnects or The Batch) for interpretation.

## What to avoid

Don't treat items as vetted recommendations -- summaries are brief and sponsored placements appear inline with editorial content (labeled, but easy to skim past). For any claim that matters, read the linked source.
