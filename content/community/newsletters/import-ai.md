## Overview

A long-running weekly newsletter about AI research, written by Jack Clark (Anthropic co-founder), combining detailed research summaries with safety and policy commentary.

## Who it's for

Researchers, practitioners, and founders who want a weekly, detail-oriented digest of AI research papers with added interpretive framing, not just headlines. Particularly useful for readers who want capability research paired with explicit safety/policy discussion rather than treated as a separate topic.

## What you'll get

- Detailed weekly summaries of specific AI research papers, often more technical than mainstream AI news coverage
- Consistent "why this matters" framing connecting individual papers to broader capability and safety trends
- A long publication history (450+ issues as of mid-2026) providing a searchable record of how specific research trends developed over time

## How to get value fast

Subscribe via Substack for the free weekly tier, which carries the core research-roundup content. Read the "why this matters" framing at the end of each item -- this interpretive layer, informed by the author's frontier-lab vantage point, is the main differentiator versus reading the underlying papers or press releases directly.

## What to avoid

Don't skip the safety and policy sections if you're only interested in capabilities -- the newsletter's editorial stance deliberately treats these as inseparable, and skipping them means missing part of the author's actual argument, not just "extra" content.
