## Overview

Nathan Lambert's newsletter covering post-training, RLHF, open models, and reasoning-model research, written from an insider vantage point (formerly Allen Institute for AI / Ai2, prior experience at Meta, DeepMind, Hugging Face).

## Who it's for

Researchers and practitioners who want technically detailed analysis of post-training methods, open-model ecosystem dynamics, and reasoning-model development -- deeper and more technical than general AI news digests like The Batch.

## What you'll get

- Technically detailed analysis of RLHF, post-training recipes, and reasoning-model techniques, often informed by direct research experience
- Ecosystem-level commentary on open-model development (e.g. comparative analysis of the US and Chinese open-model ecosystems)
- Occasional podcast-style conversations with other researchers, distinct from the written posts

## How to get value fast

Subscribe via Substack. Expect a mix of free and paid-only posts. Prioritize posts on post-training, RLHF, and open-model ecosystem topics -- this is where the author's technical depth and insider perspective add the most value versus other AI newsletters.

## What to avoid

Don't expect a fixed weekly cadence -- publishing frequency is closer to several posts per month and can vary around the author's other commitments (the author's own June 2026 "state of the blog" post explicitly discusses this).
