## Overview

AI Tinkerers is a global network of independently-run, city-based meetup chapters focused on hands-on AI building: live demos, hackathons, and workshop-format sessions rather than passive talks.

## Who it's for

Practitioners and founders who want in-person, hands-on AI-building community locally, and beginners looking for a lower-stakes way to see real AI projects built live. Best suited to people near an active chapter city, since programming and cadence vary meaningfully by location.

## What you'll get

- Hands-on, build-focused local events (live demos, hackathons, workshop-style sessions) rather than lecture-format talks
- A genuinely global footprint: chapters actively running events were confirmed in Portland, Paris, Seattle, São Paulo, Manchester (NH), Düsseldorf, and Upstate NY as of this review, among many others
- Periodic globally-synchronized events (e.g. a 24-city "OpenClaw Global Unhackathon" build day run simultaneously across the network)

## How to get value fast

Find your nearest chapter via [aitinkerers.org/events](https://aitinkerers.org/events) or a chapter-specific subdomain (e.g. `portland.aitinkerers.org`). Prioritize hands-on "build" or "hackathon" formatted events over talk-only sessions, since that hands-on format is this network's core differentiator. Watch for newly-launched chapters near you -- new chapters (e.g. a Düsseldorf inaugural meetup) continue to launch.

## What to avoid

Don't assume programming is standardized across chapters -- each is independently organized, and topic focus, cadence, and format vary by local organizer. Check a specific chapter's recent event history before assuming what you'll get.

## Activity & health

A direct fetch of multiple independent chapter pages on 2026-07-06 confirms active, scheduled programming: Portland (events through early 2026), Paris (events scheduled into July and August 2026), Seattle (a "Dev Tools" track scheduled for July 13, 2026), São Paulo (events through March 2026 and a December 2025 30-city global hackathon), Manchester NH and Upstate NY (both with July 13-14, 2026 events), and a newly-launching Düsseldorf chapter (inaugural meetup July 14, 2026). This breadth and currency across many independently-run chapters supports `activity_level: very-active`.
