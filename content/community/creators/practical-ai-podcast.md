## Overview

Practical AI is a podcast from the Changelog network covering applied AI: MLOps, LLM deployment, edge AI, and real production systems, running since 2018.

## Who it's for

Practitioners who want AI content focused on what's actually shipping in production, rather than research trends or high-level strategy -- e.g. episodes on AI agent security, document processing pipelines, and real deployment case studies.

## What you'll get

- A long-running (since 2018), consistently active show with a large back catalog (340+ episodes as of 2026)
- A recurring "Fully Connected" format where the two hosts break down current reports (e.g. the Stanford AI Index) and industry news directly
- Interviews with practitioners actually deploying AI systems, not just researchers or vendors

## How to get value fast

Look specifically for "Fully Connected" episodes if you want the hosts' own synthesis of current trends and reports. This show is part of the broader Changelog podcast network, so if you find it valuable, the wider Changelog feed covers adjacent developer-tooling topics.

## What to avoid

Don't assume every episode is a deep technical how-to -- the "Fully Connected" format episodes are higher-level trend/news discussion between the two hosts, distinct from the guest-interview episodes.
