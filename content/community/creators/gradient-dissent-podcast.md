## Overview

Gradient Dissent is Weights & Biases' podcast, hosted by cofounder Lukas Biewald, featuring conversations with ML engineers and researchers at companies like NVIDIA, Meta, and various AI labs about bringing models into production.

## Who it's for

Practitioners focused on the practical, systems side of ML/AI: training infrastructure, data tooling, and production deployment bottlenecks, told from the perspective of a company (Weights & Biases) whose own product sits in that space.

## What you'll get

- Conversations with engineers and researchers at major AI labs and infra companies about specific production ML challenges
- A long back catalog (120+ episodes as of early 2025, more added since) covering the evolution of MLOps practice
- A production-systems lens distinct from research-trend-focused podcasts

## How to get value fast

Use this show specifically for production ML-systems perspective -- the host's own background (Weights & Biases cofounder) shapes the conversation toward training/deployment bottlenecks rather than pure research trends.

## What to avoid
