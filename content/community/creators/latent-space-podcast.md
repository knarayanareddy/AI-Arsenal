## Overview

Latent Space's podcast, featuring interviews with AI engineers, founders, and researchers at labs and infrastructure companies, run by the same team as the Latent Space newsletter and AI Engineer conference series.

## Who it's for

Practitioners and founders who want long-form, technical conversations with the people actually building frontier models, agent frameworks, and AI infrastructure, rather than news-digest-style coverage.

## What you'll get

- Long-form interviews with named engineers and founders (e.g. Databricks cofounders, infrastructure company leads) discussing specific technical and product decisions
- Direct connections to the Latent Space newsletter's AI Engineer conference series, with some episodes recorded live at or around those events
- Show notes with links to referenced tools, papers, and demos

## How to get value fast

Subscribe via your podcast app of choice (RSS available through the show's Substack feed). Check episode show notes for linked code, demos, or blog posts referenced during the conversation -- many episodes accompany concrete technical announcements.

## What to avoid

Don't start here if you're new to AI engineering concepts -- episodes assume a working familiarity with the current landscape (agents, RAG, inference infra) and move quickly into specific, often company-specific technical decisions.

## Activity & health

Weekly episode cadence confirmed via a dated latest-episode check (2026-06-29, a Databricks-focused episode recorded around the Data + AI Summit), consistent with the show's established weekly release pattern (checked 2026-07-06).

## Safety & moderation

No moderation or trust concerns apply -- this is a one-way audio publication, not an interactive community space.
