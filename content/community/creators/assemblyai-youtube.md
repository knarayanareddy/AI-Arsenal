## Overview

AssemblyAI's YouTube channel, featuring applied ML/AI coding tutorials and explainers, with particular strength in the company's own core product area of speech-to-text and voice AI.

## Who it's for

Beginners and practitioners building with speech-to-text, transcription, or voice-AI APIs specifically, or those who want applied, code-along style AI tutorials more broadly.

## What you'll get

- Applied, code-along tutorial content, generally more implementation-focused than theory-focused
- Particular depth in speech-to-text, diarization, and multilingual voice-AI topics, reflecting the company's own product focus
- Content that tends to track the company's own API/product releases (e.g. Universal-3 Pro streaming features)
