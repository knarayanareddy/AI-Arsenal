## Overview

The TWIML AI Podcast (formerly "This Week in Machine Learning & AI") features long-form interviews with ML/AI researchers and practitioners on current research trends, reasoning models, robotics, and agentic systems.

## Who it's for

Practitioners and researchers who want deep, guest-led technical discussion on current ML/AI research trends, distinct from more product- or engineering-implementation-focused podcasts.

## What you'll get

- In-depth interviews with named researchers on specific technical topics (e.g. reasoning in small language models, agentic AI system design)
- A long publication history (750+ episodes as of 2026) covering the evolution of ML/AI research trends over several years
- Detailed show notes with links to referenced papers and tools

## How to get value fast

Prioritize episodes featuring named researchers or paper authors discussing a specific technical trend -- this is the show's core strength. Check the corresponding `twimlai.com/go/{episode-number}` show-notes page for links to everything referenced during the conversation.

## What to avoid

Don't expect a strict weekly cadence -- 2026 episode dates show gaps of roughly a month to six weeks between releases rather than a consistent weekly schedule.

## Activity & health

Episode dates confirmed through 2026: #762 published 2026-02-26 (featuring Sebastian Raschka on 2026 LLM trends), #763 published 2026-03-10, and #764 published 2026-04-16/17 (checked 2026-07-06). This roughly monthly-to-six-weekly cadence supports `activity_level: active` rather than `very-active`.

## Safety & moderation

No moderation or trust concerns apply -- this is a one-way audio publication, not an interactive community space.
