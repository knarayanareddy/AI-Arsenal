## Overview

fast.ai's YouTube channel, home to the "Practical Deep Learning for Coders" course series and related deep learning education content, created by Jeremy Howard and collaborators.

## Who it's for

Beginners and practitioners who want a top-down, code-first introduction to deep learning, distinct from the bottom-up, math-first approach of many university courses.

## What you'll get

- The "Practical Deep Learning for Coders" course series, a widely-referenced, code-first deep learning curriculum
- A course-based (rather than continuous-upload) content model, meaning most value is concentrated in a small number of structured series rather than spread across frequent individual videos

## How to get value fast

Start directly with the "Practical Deep Learning for Coders" course series rather than browsing individual videos -- this is fast.ai's flagship educational product and the reason most people find this channel in the first place. Treat it as a resource to work through once as a structured course, not a channel to check regularly for new content.

## What to avoid

Don't expect frequent new uploads. Evidence found during this review points to a slower, course-release-driven publishing cadence, though a confirmed dated 2026 upload could not be located during this review -- see the honesty note in Activity & health below.
