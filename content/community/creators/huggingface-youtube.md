## Overview

Hugging Face's official YouTube channel, hosting tutorials and course content on the Transformers/Diffusers ecosystem, model/dataset workflows, and Hub features (Spaces, Inference Providers).

## Who it's for

Beginners and practitioners using the Hugging Face Hub or its open-source libraries who want video-format tutorials rather than reading documentation alone.

## What you'll get

- Library- and Hub-specific tutorial content (Transformers, Diffusers, Spaces deployment, model/dataset publishing workflows)
- Course-structured playlists (e.g. agents or diffusion-focused course series) rather than only standalone videos
- Content that tracks Hub feature releases fairly closely, useful for learning new capabilities (e.g. ZeroGPU Spaces, Inference Providers) shortly after launch

## How to get value fast

Search or browse for the specific library or Hub feature you're using rather than expecting a chronological news-style feed. Check for course-linked playlists specifically, since Hugging Face frequently structures its video content around a named course rather than one-off uploads.

## What to avoid

Don't expect a fixed daily/weekly upload cadence -- like other organizational channels in this directory, publishing tracks course and feature releases rather than a calendar schedule.
