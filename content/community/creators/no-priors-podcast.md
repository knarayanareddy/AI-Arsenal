## Overview

No Priors is a weekly podcast hosted by investors Sarah Guo and Elad Gil, featuring conversations with AI founders, researchers, and engineers about frontier model development, robotics, infrastructure, and the AI investment landscape.

## Who it's for

Founders and enterprise decision-makers who want frontier-lab-adjacent perspective on where AI is heading, plus practitioners interested in robotics, infra financing, and startup-ecosystem dynamics alongside pure technical content.

## What you'll get

- Direct conversations with founders and technical leaders at major AI labs and infrastructure companies
- An annual year-ahead forecast episode aggregating predictions from a wide range of named industry figures
- Coverage extending beyond pure LLM topics into robotics, AI infrastructure financing, and adjacent biotech/deep-tech trends

## How to get value fast

Prioritize episodes featuring named founders or technical leads at labs and infra companies if you want frontier-adjacent signal specifically. Use the annual December forecast episode as a yearly checkpoint to compare predictions against what actually happened.

## What to avoid

Don't treat guest predictions as neutral analysis -- many guests are founders or investors with a direct stake in the trends they discuss. Cross-check bold specific claims (timelines, market-size predictions) independently rather than taking them at face value.

## Activity & health

Weekly cadence confirmed via a dated episode (2026-06-18, a Biohub crossover episode) and corroborated across multiple independent podcast-directory sources (podscripts.co, playpodcast.net, podscan.fm) that agree on a consistent, uninterrupted weekly release history through mid-2026 (checked 2026-07-06).

## Safety & moderation

No moderation or trust concerns apply -- this is a one-way audio publication, not an interactive community space.
