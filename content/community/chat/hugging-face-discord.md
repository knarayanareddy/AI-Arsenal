## Overview

The official Discord server for the Hugging Face open-source AI community: models, datasets, Spaces, Transformers/Diffusers library discussion, and community events (reading groups, sprints).

## Who it's for

Anyone building with open-weight models or the Hugging Face Hub -- from beginners doing their first `pip install transformers` to maintainers debugging Spaces deployment issues. Less useful if you exclusively use closed-API providers (OpenAI, Anthropic) and never touch the Hub.

## What you'll get

- Direct access to Hugging Face staff and library maintainers in topic channels (Transformers, Diffusers, Datasets, Spaces)
- Early, informal signal on Hub changes (new Inference Providers, quota changes, breaking library releases) often discussed here before a blog post exists
- Community events: reading groups, sprints, and casual "show and tell" channels for community-built Spaces and fine-tunes

## How to get value fast

Join via the official [hf.co/join/discord](https://huggingface.co/join/discord) link, then follow the in-server `#how-to-access-discord` channel's current verification steps. Introduce yourself in `#introduce-yourself` and verify your Hugging Face account before asking topic-specific questions -- verification unlocks most useful channels. Once in, skim the community-events channel to catch upcoming reading groups or sprints relevant to your interests.

## What to avoid

Old invite links circulating in blog posts, forum threads, or Stack Overflow answers from before 2024 are frequently reported as expired or broken (see the Hugging Face Forums thread "Join the Hugging Face Discord!", which has had its invite link updated multiple times since 2021). Always start from the official `hf.co/join/discord` redirect rather than a copy-pasted `discord.gg/...` link.

## Activity & health

No official public dashboard exposes Discord member or message counts. A 2026 third-party platform review (pecollective.com's LlamaIndex/Hugging Face tooling roundup) independently describes the community as "active and helpful." As a proxy signal, the adjacent, publicly browsable Hugging Face discussion forum (`discuss.huggingface.co`, a related but separate surface) shows same-day topic activity as of 2026-07-03, consistent with a broadly active overall community ecosystem (checked 2026-07-06). This is treated as `activity_level: active` rather than `very-active` specifically because the primary claim rests on indirect evidence, not a direct Discord-side measurement.

## Safety & moderation

No moderation or trust concerns were found during this review. Standard Discord verification/anti-spam gating is in place (account verification required before most channels unlock).
