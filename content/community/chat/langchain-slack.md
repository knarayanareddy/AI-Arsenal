## Overview

LangChain's official real-time community chat, run on Slack. This replaced LangChain's original Discord server, which was permanently deleted on November 1, 2024.

## Who it's for

Developers building with LangChain or LangGraph who want real-time chat with other builders, Community Champions, and occasional LangChain team presence. Founders and teams evaluating LangChain for production use will also find the Ambassador/Champion network here useful for finding case studies and experienced users.

## What you'll get

- Real-time help in dedicated channels for LangChain, LangGraph, and general "talking shop" agent/RAG discussion
- Visibility into the Community Champions program (contributors with direct access to the LangChain team and roadmap influence) and the geographically distributed Ambassador network
- Announcements about events, meetups, and workshops (cross-linked to LangChain's public events calendar)

## How to get value fast

Sign up through the official [langchain.com/join-community](https://www.langchain.com/join-community) page. Once in, use the channels a LangChain team member has directly named as active: `#i-made-this`, `#langchain-help`, and `#langgraph-help`, rather than posting in a generic catch-all channel. If you want deeper, ongoing involvement rather than occasional Q&A, review the Community Champions and Ambassador programs listed on LangChain's community page.

## What to avoid

Do not use old `discord.gg/...` invite links still circulating in blog posts, GitHub discussions, and Stack Overflow answers from before November 2024 -- LangChain's original Discord server was permanently deleted on 2024-11-01 (confirmed via multiple independently dated Reddit threads) and the community fully migrated to this Slack. Any Discord invite you find for "LangChain community" predating that date is dead.

## Activity & health

LangChain's own current community page (fetched directly, 2026-07-06) actively promotes this Slack as its primary real-time chat surface, distinct from the separate LangChain Forum (see [LangChain Forum](../forums/langchain-forum.md)). The prior Discord-to-Slack migration is corroborated by multiple independently dated Reddit threads spanning 2024-2025, and the current community page (as of this check) makes no mention of a Discord at all, consistent with the migration being permanent rather than temporary. No public message-volume or member-count metric is exposed for the Slack itself, so this is rated `active` rather than `very-active`.

## Safety & moderation

No moderation or trust concerns were found during this review.

## Relation to the Arsenal

Paired with [LangChain Forum](../forums/langchain-forum.md) (LangChain's separate, asynchronous, Discourse-based help forum) -- use this Slack for real-time chat and the forum for searchable, indexed Q&A.
