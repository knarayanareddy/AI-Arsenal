# Trending

Curated signals about what is moving in the AI engineering ecosystem.

## Status

**Partially populated; automated generation queued.** Trending is now a
first-class entry type: `entry_type: "trend"` validated by
`schemas/trend.schema.json`, with three `kind`s — `weekly-snapshot`,
`hall-of-fame`, and `source-feed`. Human-curated entries exist
([Hall of Fame](./hall-of-fame.md), [This Week in AI Arsenal](./this-week.md),
and two [by-source](./by-source/) feeds). Automated weekly generation is
driven by `scripts/draft-trending.js` (which emits the `trend` schema) from
`github_stars` velocity, buzz sources, and recency, and published by the
`weekly.yml` workflow. Until then, trending is maintained by hand, and every
snapshot carries an `as_of` date plus dated `sources`.
