# Evaluation & Observability Tools

## What belongs here

Evaluation frameworks, tracing, monitoring, security/guardrail scanning, drift detection, and logging.

## What does NOT belong here

Data labeling for training data belongs in Data Ingestion; this phase is about judging behavior after the fact.

## Decision guidance

Before picking a tool in this phase, consider:

- See [Architecture Decision Trees](../../architectures/decision-trees/_index.md) for cross-cutting guidance.
- Key question to ask: Does this tool tell you whether your AI system is working correctly, safely, or efficiently?
